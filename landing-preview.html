How it works

Five steps. One signature per milestone.

  1. 01

    Describe.

    Chat the deal in plain language.

  2. 02

    Negotiate.

    Agents counter on both sides. You accept the summary.

  3. 03

    Fund.

    One signature. USDC enters the on-chain vault.

  4. 04

    Verify.

    Seller uploads proof. Verifier scores it.

  5. 05

    Release.

    One tap. That milestone pays. Not the pot.

Recovery path, also shipped.

Mutual refund returns the remaining USDC when both sides sign. Pre-funding deals cancel off-chain.
