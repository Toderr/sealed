## Slide 2 · Problem

### Business deals break. Both sides lose.

**The scale.** **50%** of global B2B invoices are overdue in 2025. **$15B/year** lost to freelancers alone. **25%** of EU bankruptcies are caused by late payments.

**The trigger.** **70%** of non-payment cases are disputes over what was delivered. It's not a payment problem. It's a *verification problem*. Bare escrow locks funds, but it can't verify delivery.

The money moves. The trust doesn't.

*Sources: Atradius 2025 B2B Payment Practices Barometer · Flexable 2025 Global Freelancer Non-Payment Report.*

---

## Slide 3 · Solution

### Three AI agents. One deal table.

**Structurer** parses the deal. **Negotiator** reaches terms for both sides. **Verifier** reviews delivery.

Funds lock on-chain. Release on milestone. Mutual refund if it breaks.

No lawyer. No bank. No dispute hotline.

Just code that can't lie.
