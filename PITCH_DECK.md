## Slide 2 — Problem

### 64 million Indonesian MSMEs close deals on trust alone.

Indonesia's B2B ecommerce market is projected to reach ~$14B in 2026 and grow at 18.74% CAGR through 2031. Most of it still settles over WhatsApp and bank transfers.

- No escrow.
- No enforceable milestones.
- No shared source of truth.

When a deal goes wrong, the options are ugly: eat the loss, hire a lawyer, or send a debt collector.

**One real quote from our community:** *"I shipped 40% of the order. The buyer ghosted. I have no recourse."*

*Sources: Mordor Intelligence, trade.gov (Indonesia ecommerce 2026).*

---

## Slide 3 — Solution

### An AI agent that represents your business on an on-chain deal table.

1. **Chat** — describe your deal in plain language. Indonesian or English.
2. **Structure** — the agent converts it into milestones, amounts, and release conditions.
3. **Sign** — both parties approve. Funds lock in a Solana smart contract.
4. **Settle** — milestones release automatically on delivery confirmation.

No lawyer. No bank. No dispute hotline.

Just code that can't lie.
